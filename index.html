Executive Summary

Solar and wind power are growing quickly, capturing attention and investment, while geothermal quietly delivers impressive efficiency. This report by Green Energy takes a closer look at how these energy sources are performing, highlights the trends shaping the industry, and offers practical insights for anyone interested in building a cleaner, more sustainable future.
